18 DISPLAY DATE IN SPECIFIED FORMAT : Ninth September, 2021 Thursday

19 DISPLAY DATE IN SPECIFIED FORMAT : Today is 5th day of week,9th day of September and 252nd day of year Twenty Twenty-One.
-> SELECT SYSDATE, 'To day is ' || TO_CHAR(SYSDATE, 'DTH') || ' day of the week, '  || TO_CHAR(SYSDATE, 'DDTH') || ' day of ' || TO_CHAR(SYSDATE, 'Month') || ' and ' || TO_CHAR(SYSDATE, 'DDDTH') || ' day of year ' || TO_CHAR(SYSDATE, 'Year') || '.' FROM DUAL;

20 DISPLAY DATE IN SPECIFIED FORMAT : Next year will start after ... days.
-> SELECT 'Next year will start after' || TO_DATE('31-12-22', 'DD-MM-RR') - SYSDATE || 'days.' FROM DUAL;
